
YALLA 
The language learning site where you can learn Arabic, both as a beginner and on an intermediate level, and learn about relevant cultural points to fully round out and supplement your learning.

About the creator

My name is Fatima Jassim, and I started learning languages at a young age. My family taught me the alphabet, and when I started attending Arabic school I began to read, write, and understand some of the Standard Arabic. In middle school, I discovered French through an online language learning website and I completely fell in love. I began to take French classes at my school, befriended a French penpal, and consumed French news, cartoons, and books. However, when I tried to apply the same strategies to Arabic, I was at a loss. The few Arabic learning sites hardly went beyond beginner vocabulary, or focused mainly on grammar. There were Arabic dubbed cartoons that I could watch, but the huge lack of Arabic learning resources really got to me. Additionally, seeing over the years how there are many misunderstandings about Arab culture, I began to dream of a website that taught both Arabic and Arab culture well, in a fun and easy way.

Thus, yalla was born!

The word 'yalla' is an Arabic phrase that means "Come on!" or "Let's Go!" I thought it very fitting for a language learning site where everyone is on a learning journey :)
